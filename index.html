1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how TSK handles information when you use the app. TSK does not operate a developer-owned server, cloud database, or account system for storing your personal data. The app is built to keep your information on your device by default.

If you enable iCloud sync, some supported app data may be stored in your private iCloud account so it can stay available across your devices signed into the same Apple ID.

2. Information TSK May Store

Depending on the features you use, TSK may store locally on your device:

  • Tasks, reminders, schedules, notes, and subtasks
  • Work and school profiles, classes, assignments, and related planning data
  • Finance information such as bills, expenses, receipts, merchants, totals, and notes
  • Vehicle, mileage, trip, fuel, and geofence-related data
  • Sleep summaries and wellness views derived from Apple Health data you choose to share
  • App settings, preferences, and feature toggles
  • Local learning data used to improve reminders and automation behavior on device

3. How We Use Information

TSK uses information only to provide app features you choose to use, including:

  • Planning and managing tasks, reminders, schedules, and recurring events
  • Tracking work, school, finance, sleep, and vehicle information
  • Detecting drives, trips, commutes, steps, or geofence events when enabled
  • Scanning, saving, and organizing receipt images when you choose to do so
  • Syncing data through iCloud if you enable sync
  • Sending notifications you ask the app to send

4. Permissions We May Request

TSK may ask for the following permissions only when a feature requires them:

  • Location for drive tracking, commute features, and geofencing
  • Motion/Fitness data for drive detection, walking detection, and activity-based automation
  • Apple Health data for sleep insights and related wellness summaries
  • Camera for receipt scanning and document capture
  • Photos for choosing or saving receipt images
  • Notifications for reminders and alerts
You can disable any permission in device settings at any time. Some features may stop working if permission is removed.

5. What TSK Does Not Do

  • TSK does not sell your personal information.
  • TSK does not use advertising trackers or ad networks.
  • TSK does not maintain a developer-owned database of your data.
  • TSK does not require an account to use the app.
  • TSK does not collect your data for our own servers in the current release.

6. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, supported data may be stored in your private iCloud database and synced across devices signed into the same Apple ID. This is handled through Apple’s services, not a TSK-owned backend.

If iCloud sync is turned off, TSK continues to work as a local-only app.

7. Health Data

If you grant Health access, TSK may read selected sleep-related or heart-related data from Apple Health to power insights and summaries. That data is used to display information inside the app. TSK does not maintain a separate health-data database of its own.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Because TSK is local-first, your data remains on your device unless you delete it, disable sync, or remove the app. If iCloud sync is enabled, synced content may remain in your private iCloud account until you delete it from your devices or from iCloud.

You can remove data inside the app, turn off permissions in system settings, disable iCloud sync, or delete the app to remove local app data.

9. Security

TSK is designed to minimize data exposure by keeping data on your device whenever possible. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we aim to avoid collecting or hosting your personal data on developer-controlled infrastructure in the first place.

10. Children’s Privacy

TSK is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
