Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April, 10 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TSK ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use the app. TSK is designed to work primarily on your device, and most of your data stays on your device unless you choose to enable optional syncing or permissions-based features.

Information We Collect

TSK may store the following types of information, depending on the features you use:

  • Tasks, reminders, schedules, notes, subtasks, and related planning data
  • Work and school profile information you enter
  • Finance data such as bills, expenses, receipts, merchants, totals, and categories
  • Vehicle and trip data such as drive history, mileage, destinations, fuel logs, and geofence-related records
  • Sleep-related data and wellness insights you choose to view through Apple Health
  • App preferences, settings, and feature choices
  • Diagnostic or learning data that helps the app improve reminders, timing, and suggestions on your device

How Information Is Collected

We collect information in these ways:

  • Information you enter directly into the app
  • Information the app generates locally while you use it
  • Information received from Apple frameworks or device permissions you enable, such as location, motion, photos, camera, notifications, and Apple Health
  • Optional iCloud sync if you choose to enable it

How We Use Information

We use information only to provide and improve TSK’s features, including:

  • Planning tasks, schedules, and reminders
  • Tracking work, school, vehicle, finance, and sleep information
  • Automatically detecting drives, trips, commutes, or other motion-related events when enabled
  • Saving receipt images and scanned receipt data when you choose to use those features
  • Syncing your data across devices through iCloud if enabled
  • Showing notifications you ask the app to send
  • Improving the app’s local learning and automation features

Permissions We May Request

Depending on the features you turn on, TSK may request access to:

  • Location for drive tracking, commute features, and geofencing
  • Motion/Fitness data for drive detection, walking detection, and related automations
  • Apple Health data for sleep insights and related wellness features
  • Camera for receipt scanning or document capture
  • Photos for selecting or saving receipt images
  • Notifications for reminders and alerts

You can disable these permissions at any time in your device settings, although some features may stop working.

Where Your Data Is Stored

By default, most TSK data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, some supported data may also be stored in your private iCloud account so it can sync across your devices signed into the same Apple ID.

Third Parties

TSK does not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising networks. If you enable iCloud sync, Apple may process and store your synced data as part of its iCloud services. If future versions of TSK include optional cloud-based features such as crash reporting or support tools, we will update this policy to explain what data is shared and why.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device unless you delete it, turn off syncing, or remove the app. If iCloud sync is enabled, synced data may remain in your iCloud account until you delete it from your devices or from iCloud.

Your Choices and Controls

You can control your data in several ways:

  • Turn permissions on or off in device settings
  • Disable iCloud sync in the app or device settings
  • Delete tasks, receipts, trips, health-related views, or other content inside the app
  • Delete the app to remove local data stored on the device

Children’s Privacy

TSK is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date.
